What is SkillBridge?

SkillBridge is a two-sided freelance marketplace where clients post real projects and skilled freelancers apply with tailored proposals. Whether you're a startup building your first product or an experienced developer looking for meaningful work, SkillBridge connects the right talent to the right opportunity — fast, transparently, and with zero noise.

How does SkillBridge work?

Clients post a project describing their requirements, budget, and timeline. Freelancers browse open projects and submit proposals with their approach and pricing. Clients review proposals, check freelancer profiles and portfolios, and hire the best match — all in one place. Communication and contracts happen directly on the platform.

Who is SkillBridge for?

SkillBridge is for businesses of every size — from solo founders to growing product teams — that need flexible access to specialized expertise. It's equally built for skilled professionals (developers, designers, writers, data scientists) who want to find meaningful projects, build a strong portfolio, and grow their freelance careers on their own terms.

Is posting a project free?

Your first project post is completely free. After that, a small listing fee applies — this keeps the quality of projects high and ensures freelancers spend their time on serious opportunities. Freelancer profiles and proposals are always free to create and submit.
