Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use SkillBridge:

  • Account data: Your name, email address, and password (hashed).
  • Profile data: Skills, bio, hourly rate, portfolio links, and avatar.
  • Activity data: Projects posted, proposals submitted, messages sent.
  • Device data: Browser type, IP address, and usage patterns via analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your profile to clients and other users of the platform.
  • To match clients with relevant freelancers based on skills.
  • To send transactional emails (e.g. proposal confirmations, payment receipts).
  • To improve the platform through aggregated, anonymized usage analytics.

3. Data Storage

SkillBridge is currently a client-side application. Your data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We do not transmit personal data to external servers at this time. This means your data remains on your device.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your public profile (name, skills, bio, portfolio) is visible to other logged-in users. Your email and password are never shared with third parties.

5. Cookies

We use localStorage (a browser storage technology) to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all profile data in your account settings.
  • Correction: You can update your information at any time via the profile edit page.

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Passwords are not stored in plain text. However, no online system is completely secure — please use a strong, unique password.

Community Guidelines

Last updated: June 2026

SkillBridge is a professional marketplace. We expect all users to treat each other with respect and integrity.

Be Honest

Only list skills, experience, and portfolios that accurately represent your abilities. Misrepresentation will result in account suspension.

Be Professional

Communicate with clients and freelancers in a respectful, professional manner. Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language will not be tolerated.

Protect Confidentiality

Respect the confidentiality of projects and information shared with you. Do not share proprietary client data outside the engagement.

No Spam

Do not send unsolicited messages or submit generic, copy-paste proposals. Quality over quantity earns better results for everyone.

No Off-Platform Transactions

Arranging payments outside the SkillBridge platform circumvents our protections for both parties. This violates our Terms and may result in account termination.

Report Violations

If you encounter behavior that violates these Guidelines, use the "Report" feature or contact us at support@skillbridge.in
